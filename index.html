Welcome to Green PicNic

A game for making sustainable choices

Aim of the game

It's finally the weekend and it's a beautiful day for you and your dog to go out. You have decided that you would like to meet your friend at the park for a picnic.
Your tasks are to prepare the picnic basket, travel to the park and then dispose of any waste you have from the picnic. Throughout the game, your choices will affect the environental factors known as Carbon Footprint, Water Pollution, Habitat Destructionand Waste. It is your job to make sure the environmental factors are as low as possible whilst getting to the park to enjoy your picnic with a friend.
